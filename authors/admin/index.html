Yifei Liu

Ph.D. Candidate of Computer Science

Stony Brook University

I am Yifei Liu, a rising fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Stony Brook University . I am part of the File systems and Storage Lab (FSL) advised by Prof. Erez Zadok . I am broadly interested in file/storage systems and operating systems. My recent works seek to test kernel file systems more easily and thoroughly by model checking and other related techniques.

Before Stony Brook, I was a Master’s student and research assistant at Wuhan National Laboratory for Optoelectronics advised by Prof. Ke Zhou at Huazhong University of Science and Technology , where I worked on performance improvement for content-based semantic queries in storage systems.

Interests

  • Operating Systems
  • Model Checking and Software Testing
